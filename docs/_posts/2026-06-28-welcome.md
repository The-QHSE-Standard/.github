title: Why this site exists
subtitle: A hub for the people who do the work, not just the people who write the procedures.
date: 2026-06-28
---

Most safety communication is written for the audit. It describes
**Work-as-Imagined**&nbsp;&mdash; the clean version of the job that lives in the
procedure. The trouble is that nobody actually works in the imagination. They work
in **Work-as-Done**: the messy, adaptive, resource-constrained reality where things
mostly go right *because* people adjust, improvise, and fill the gaps the procedure
left open.

This site is the front door to that idea. The full library lives at
[qhsestandard.com]({{ site.website }}); here we keep things short&nbsp;&mdash; a place
to point people, a feed to subscribe to, and an easy way to
[flag what we got wrong]({{ site.github_org }}/.github/issues/new/choose).

If you&rsquo;re new, the fastest way in is
[Start here]({{ '/start-here/' | relative_url }}): four reads that tend to change how
people think about their own safety record.

> Stop auditing the imagination. Start understanding the reality.
