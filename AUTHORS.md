## Scope & limitations

We are transparent about the boundaries of our expertise — this is itself a
safety principle.

- Our role is **strategic and curatorial oversight**, grounded in 20+ years of
  digital strategy and platform architecture.
- We communicate **established safety-science paradigms** for learning and
  discussion. We do **not** provide bespoke operational safety advice.
- We are **not accredited Human Factors/Ergonomics specialists or Professional
  Engineers**. For decisions specific to your workplace, consult a qualified
  professional in your jurisdiction.

This honesty is deliberate: authority is built on knowing — and stating — where
your competence ends.
